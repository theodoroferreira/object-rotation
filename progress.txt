## Codebase Patterns
- PyOpenGL has no type stubs; mypy.ini configured with `ignore_missing_imports = True` for OpenGL.*
- Camera uses spherical coordinates (distance, azimuth, elevation) converted to cartesian for gluLookAt
- GLUT double-buffered with RGB and depth buffer: `GLUT_DOUBLE | GLUT_RGB | GLUT_DEPTH`
